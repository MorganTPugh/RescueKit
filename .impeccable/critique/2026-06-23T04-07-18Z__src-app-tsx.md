## Anti-Patterns Verdict

LLM: RescueKit avoids the worst AI slop traps. The emoji nav tabs, Fraunces serif heading, and warm brand voice are genuinely differentiating. The sky/indigo palette sits in the "caring helpful tool" zone but doesn't read as generic. The bigger concerns are structural: duplicate content blocks, competing value-prop messaging, and a layout that grew feature by feature.

Detector: 12 findings — 11 gray-on-color warnings (partial false positives on contrast; real visual coherence concern on inactive nav text-slate-600 on sky-100), 1 ai-color-palette false positive on logo wordmark.

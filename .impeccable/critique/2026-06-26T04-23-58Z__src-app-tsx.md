## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: The app avoids generic slop. The paw + sparkle brand header, Space Grotesk type accents, and Fraunces title fonts create a warm, custom aesthetic. The layout hierarchy is clear, responsive, and fits perfectly on both mobile edit tabs and desktop split screens.

**Deterministic scan**: Detected 1 warning in `src/App.tsx` and 11 warnings in `src/components/RescueForms.tsx`. All are `gray-on-color` findings where gray text (`text-slate-600` or `text-slate-800`) is rendered on colored backgrounds (`bg-indigo-50` or `bg-sky-100`). This causes slight legibility loss on inactive items or dropdown selections.

## Overall Impression
The RescueKit site is highly functional and visually cohesive. The new **Rescue Forms** tab fits seamlessly into the workspace, offering an intuitive customization tool for rescue volunteers. The layout is optimized, but small tweaks to color harmony and inline helpers would refine the UX.

## What's Working
1. **Realistic Paper Preview**: The letter-sized canvas preview offers an excellent WYSIWYG experience for print documents.
2. **Stepper Layout**: Stepper layouts on mobile keep multi-page forms digestibly short.
3. **Autosave and State Persistence**: Storing user details prevents accidental data loss.

## Priority Issues

### [P2] Inactive text gray-on-color legibility
* **Why it matters**: Slate text on sky/indigo backdrops violates color harmony and reduces readability.
* **Fix**: Change text classes from `text-slate-600` or `text-slate-800` to a deeper hue-matched shade like `text-sky-800` or `text-indigo-800`.
* **Suggested command**: `/impeccable colorize`

### [P2] Photos not persisted to localStorage
* **Why it matters**: Users reloading the page lose custom uploaded logos or hero images without an explicit warn/save banner.
* **Fix**: Store base64 data strings for images in localStorage or warn users.
* **Suggested command**: `/impeccable harden`

### [P3] Limited inline contextual help
* **Why it matters**: First-time volunteers filling out complex intake exams might be confused by veterinary terms.
* **Fix**: Add small tooltip elements next to specialized fields.
* **Suggested command**: `/impeccable onboard`
